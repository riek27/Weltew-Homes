About Juba Auto Town

Excellence in automotive retail and service since 2010

Redefining Automotive Excellence

Juba Auto Town stands as the premier destination for luxury and performance vehicles in South Sudan. Founded in 2010, we've built a reputation for excellence, integrity, and unparalleled customer service over the past decade.

Our showroom features an exquisite collection of the world's most sought-after automotive brands, each meticulously selected to meet the discerning tastes of our clientele.

Beyond sales, our comprehensive services include vehicle maintenance, customization, and financing solutions, ensuring a seamless ownership experience from start to finish.
