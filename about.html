Our Story

Founded with a vision to transform the construction landscape in South Sudan, AD&B Construction Co. Ltd has been at the forefront of innovative building technologies and sustainable construction practices since our inception.

Specializing in Interlocking Stabilized Soil Blocks (ISSB), we've pioneered cost-effective and eco-friendly construction methods that have revolutionized the building industry. Our technology makes quality housing more accessible while significantly reducing environmental impact.

With a dedicated team of experienced architects, engineers, and construction professionals, we deliver comprehensive end-to-end solutions from initial design concepts to project completion. We are committed to quality, durability, and creating lasting value for our clients and communities.

Our Mission

To provide innovative, sustainable, and cost-effective construction solutions that transform communities and improve living standards through advanced building technologies and exceptional service.

Our Vision

To be the leading construction company in East Africa, recognized for excellence in sustainable building practices, innovative construction technologies, and community development.
