Interlocking Stabilized Soil Blocks (ISSB Production)

Our ISSB technology revolutionizes construction by providing an affordable, sustainable, and efficient alternative to conventional building materials. This innovative method combines local soil with a small amount of cement to create strong, interlocking blocks that require minimal mortar.

  • Significant Cost Savings: ISSB saves up to 40% of construction costs compared to fired bricks or concrete blocks, making quality housing more accessible.
  • Eco-Friendly Construction: Made from locally available soil with minimal cement (5-10%), significantly reducing carbon footprint and environmental impact.
  • Superior Durability: High compressive strength (up to 7MPa) with excellent thermal and acoustic properties for comfortable living spaces.
  • Construction Efficiency: Interlocking design eliminates the need for mortar in 70% of joints, speeding up construction by up to 50%.
  • Versatile Applications: Suitable for residential, commercial, institutional, and agricultural buildings of all sizes.
  • Minimal Skill Requirement: Simple construction technique that can be mastered quickly, creating local employment opportunities.
