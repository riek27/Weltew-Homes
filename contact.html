Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery within Juba takes 3-5 business days. For custom orders, delivery time varies based on the complexity of the piece.

For standard items in stock, we aim to deliver within 3-5 business days in Juba. For areas outside Juba, delivery may take 7-10 business days depending on location.

Custom furniture orders require additional time for crafting. Typically, these take 4-8 weeks from order confirmation to delivery. We'll provide a specific timeline when you place your custom order.

All deliveries include professional installation and setup at no additional cost. We'll coordinate with you to schedule a convenient delivery time.

Do you offer custom furniture design?

Yes, we specialize in creating bespoke furniture pieces tailored to your specific requirements and space.

Absolutely! Custom furniture design is one of our specialties. We work closely with you to create pieces that perfectly fit your space, style, and functional needs.

Our design process begins with a consultation to understand your vision, requirements, and budget. We then create detailed designs and provide material samples for your approval.

Once the design is finalized, our master craftsmen bring your vision to life using traditional techniques and premium materials. We maintain communication throughout the process to ensure the final product exceeds your expectations.

Custom projects typically take 4-8 weeks from design approval to completion, depending on complexity. We provide regular updates on progress throughout the crafting process.

What is your warranty policy?

We offer comprehensive warranties on all our furniture, with coverage varying by product type and materials.

We stand behind the quality of our craftsmanship and materials. All Weltew Homes furniture comes with a warranty against manufacturing defects.

Wood furniture: 10-year warranty on frames and 5-year warranty on finishes

Upholstered furniture: 7-year warranty on frames and 2-year warranty on cushions and fabrics

Our warranty covers repairs or replacement of defective parts. It does not cover damage from misuse, accidents, or normal wear and tear.

For warranty claims, please contact our customer service team with photos of the issue and your original purchase information. We'll guide you through the process and arrange for inspection and repair if needed.

Do you provide interior design services?

Yes, we offer professional interior design consultation to help you create cohesive and beautiful living spaces.

Our interior design services help you create spaces that reflect your personality while maximizing functionality and aesthetic appeal.
