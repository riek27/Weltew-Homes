Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How much can I save with ISSB construction?

ISSB construction can save you up to 40% compared to conventional building methods. This includes savings on materials (less cement required), labor (faster construction), and transportation (using locally available soil). A complete 3-bedroom house typically costs between $9,000 - $13,000 using our ISSB technology.

How long does it take to build a house with ISSB?

A standard 3-bedroom house can be completed in 6-8 weeks using our ISSB method, compared to 3-4 months with conventional construction. The interlocking design eliminates the need for mortar in most joints, significantly speeding up the construction process.

Do you provide financing options?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for our construction projects. We can structure payments according to project milestones, making it easier to manage your budget. We also work with local financial institutions to help our clients secure construction loans.

What areas do you serve?

We are based in Juba, South Sudan, and serve clients throughout the country. We have completed projects in various regions and are equipped to handle construction projects anywhere in South Sudan. For international inquiries, please contact us directly to discuss your specific requirements.
